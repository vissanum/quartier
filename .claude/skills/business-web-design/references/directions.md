# Design directions

Eight directions tuned to local-business trades. Each locks concrete starting tokens so
the aesthetic is a commitment, not a vibe. Pick ONE per project; never blend two.

## How to use

1. Map the trade with the table below; when two directions fit, pick the one whose
   **color world** better matches the business's existing brand.
2. Rotate type AND hero topology: check the 2-3 most recent projects
   (`projects/*/redesign/` design-lock comments) and don't repeat their display fonts or
   their hero topology. If recent projects exhaust a direction's pool (three fisios in a
   row), switch to the trade's ALTERNATE direction rather than repeating a font.
3. Brand colors always win over the direction's palette family — the direction tells you
   how to *treat* them (temperature, contrast, where the accent goes), not what they are.
4. All fonts below are on Google Fonts (the project serves static sites with no build).
   Excluded from every pool because they have drifted into AI-default territory (we
   measured Fraunces in 3 of 6 eval outputs): **Fraunces, Playfair Display, DM Serif**.

## Hero topology menu

Lock ONE in the design-lock. The split hero is allowed but it is the layout the model
flees to when the centered hero is banned — choosing it must be a decision, not a reflex,
and it can never repeat two projects in a row.

| Topology | What it is | Naturally fits |
|---|---|---|
| Poster typographic | Type IS the image; huge display font, no visual block | Ink & Craft, Bold Local, Industrial Honest |
| Full-bleed overlay | Photo/texture edge to edge, type composed on top | Editorial Warm, Mediterranean Light |
| Editorial column | One centered-but-asymmetric reading column, generous margins, no side visual | Clinical Calm, Quiet Luxury |
| Split (unequal) | Text and visual side by side at 60/40 or 70/30 — never 50/50 | any, max 1 per 3 projects |
| Broken grid | Overlapping blocks, type crossing image edges, off-axis focal point | Ink & Craft, Bold Local, Mercado Fresco |
| Object stage | One product/object/illustration staged center-large, type orbiting | Quiet Luxury, Mercado Fresco |

| Trade | First choice | Alternate |
|---|---|---|
| Restaurante, bar, cafetería | Editorial Warm | Mercado Fresco |
| Panadería, pastelería, artesanía | Mercado Fresco | Editorial Warm |
| Fisioterapia, clínica, psicología | Clinical Calm | Quiet Luxury |
| Peluquería, barbería, tatuaje | Ink & Craft | Bold Local |
| Taller, ferretería, construcción | Industrial Honest | Bold Local |
| Hotel, casa rural, turismo | Mediterranean Light | Editorial Warm |
| Óptica, joyería, boutique | Quiet Luxury | Mediterranean Light |
| Gimnasio, academia, club deportivo | Bold Local | Industrial Honest |
| Abogado, asesoría, gestoría | Quiet Luxury (sober variant) | Clinical Calm |
| Yoga, pilates, floristería | Mercado Fresco (soft variant) | Clinical Calm |

## Editorial Warm

For trades where appetite and atmosphere sell. Feels like a food magazine spread.

- **Type pool — display:** Petrona, Lora, Bitter, Gelasio.
  **Body:** Source Serif 4, Karla, PT Sans.
- **Palette derivation:** deep warm ink (espresso/burgundy family) on warm paper; accent
  from the food/room itself (tomato, saffron, olive). Dark variant: candlelit — warm
  near-black, cream type.
- **Topology:** full-bleed photography heroes; alternating image/text at unequal ratios
  (60/40, 70/30); menu/services as typographic lists with dot leaders, not cards.
- **Motion (5-7):** slow ken-burns on hero, reveals that feel like page-turns, hover
  states that warm (color, not scale).
- **Texture:** subtle paper grain; hairline rules between menu items.
- **Avoid inside this direction:** script fonts, color-overlay-on-photo heroes, more than
  one photo treatment.

## Mercado Fresco

For fresh-product and handmade trades. Feels like a chalkboard and brown paper.

- **Type pool — display:** Bricolage Grotesque, Hanken Grotesk (800+), Sniglet sparingly.
  **Body:** Cabin, Mulish.
- **Palette derivation:** produce colors at full saturation as accents (one only) on
  flour/kraft canvases; greens that are vegetal, not corporate.
- **Topology:** product photography in organic crops (circles, arches); handwritten-style
  annotations as the signature (one, not ten); price tags as typographic objects.
- **Motion (4-6):** items that settle into place (small translate + settle easing),
  marquee strip for hours/market days.
- **Texture:** kraft paper, slight rotation (±1.5°) on photo cards — imperfection as
  authenticity, used twice per page maximum.
- **Avoid:** rounded-everything cuteness, emoji, pastel gradients.

## Clinical Calm

For health trades. Calm and competent without the generic medical-blue freeze.

- **Type pool — display:** Instrument Sans (600+), Albert Sans, Schibsted Grotesk.
  **Body:** Atkinson Hyperlegible (a11y story built in), Mulish.
- **Palette derivation:** skin-warm neutrals as canvas; one clinical accent drawn from
  the brand (teal, clay, sage — NOT default soft blue unless the brand owns it); high
  text contrast (this audience skews older).
- **Topology:** generous whitespace, single-column rhythm with wide breathing room;
  treatments as an editorial numbered list (large numerals in display font); team photos
  large and honest.
- **Motion (2-4):** minimal — soft reveals, a breathing CTA. Calm IS the brand.
- **Signature ideas:** anatomical line illustration as section dividers; appointment-card
  styled CTA block.
- **Avoid:** stock hands/spine photos, icon grids of body parts, blue gradients.

## Ink & Craft

For barbershops, tattoo studios, hair salons with attitude. Feels like a flash sheet.

- **Type pool — display:** Archivo Black, Anton, Big Shoulders. **Body:** Archivo,
  IBM Plex Sans.
- **Palette derivation:** near-black ink + bone paper; one pigment accent (oxblood,
  bottle green, mustard) from the shop's interior or logo.
- **Topology:** poster-like hero (type as image); price list as a typographic board;
  gallery as a contact sheet (uneven grid, captions).
- **Motion (6-8):** confident — type that slides in like a stamp, images that develop
  (clip-path reveals), hover inverts (ink/paper swap).
- **Texture:** halftone or risograph grain on photos; hairline borders, zero radius.
- **Avoid:** soft shadows, rounded cards, anything timid.

## Industrial Honest

For workshops and trades. Feels like a well-organized toolbox: nothing decorative lies.

- **Type pool — display:** Saira Condensed (700+), Barlow Condensed, Oswald.
  **Body:** Barlow, IBM Plex Sans.
- **Palette derivation:** steel/asphalt neutrals; safety accent (amber, signal red) used
  ONLY for the primary action; brand color if the sign has one.
- **Topology:** data-forward — services as a spec table (the signature: set it like a
  parts catalog), big phone number treated as display type, map prominent.
- **Motion (3-5):** mechanical precision — short distances, fast decel, no float.
- **Texture:** stamped-plate label for the "since 1986" badge; diagonal hazard rule used
  once.
- **Avoid:** gradients entirely, lifestyle stock, any italic.

## Mediterranean Light

For hospitality and places. Feels like 11am light on plaster.

- **Type pool — display:** Cormorant Garamond (600+), Marcellus, Italiana (sparingly,
  large sizes only). **Body:** Figtree, Nunito Sans.
- **Palette derivation:** plaster white (warm, never pure), sea/tile accent from the
  place itself, terracotta or olive as secondary; photography carries color.
- **Topology:** airy editorial — large photography with generous margins; itinerary/rooms
  as a vertical timeline; arch-shaped image masks as the signature (sparingly).
- **Motion (4-6):** unhurried — slow parallax on landscape photos, soft crossfades.
- **Avoid:** beige-luxury sameness (differentiate via the place's actual colors),
  script fonts, dark overlays on photos.

## Quiet Luxury

For boutiques, jewelers, opticians, law firms (sober variant). Whisper, never shout.

- **Type pool — display:** Cormorant (500-600), STIX Two Text, Libre Caslon Text (also
  the sober variant's pick). **Body:** Jost, Outfit (300-400).
- **Palette derivation:** two-color discipline — deep ink + paper, with at most a metal
  tone (brass, graphite) as whisper accent. The sober variant: navy-black + cool paper.
- **Topology:** extreme negative space; one object per viewport; product/service detail
  at large scale; footer as a letterhead.
- **Motion (2-4):** barely-there — opacity and 4-8px distances, long ease-outs. A single
  refined moment (e.g. a lens flare across a product on first load) as signature.
- **Avoid:** the beige+brass+oxblood "premium" cliché combo, glassmorphism, more than
  one accent.

## Bold Local

For gyms, academies, clubs, anything with energy and community.

- **Type pool — display:** Lexend (800+), Sora (700+), Big Shoulders. **Body:** Lexend
  (300-400), Inter Tight is BANNED (too close to Inter) — use Albert Sans.
- **Palette derivation:** the club's colors at full commitment — dominant dark canvas,
  brand color as large surface (not just accents); duotone photo treatment in brand
  colors as the signature.
- **Topology:** oversized type (numbers: schedules, prices, distances) as the visual
  system; diagonal section cuts (one, maybe two); schedule as a real timetable grid.
- **Motion (6-8):** energetic — staggered type reveals, counters that count, marquee of
  activities; still respects the degradation triple.
- **Avoid:** neon-on-charcoal dev-tool look, motivational stock ("no pain no gain"),
  more than two diagonal cuts.

## Adding a direction

Copy this template, fill every field with concrete values (a direction without locked
tokens is a mood board, not a direction), and add the trade mapping above:

```
## <Name>
For <trades>. Feels like <one concrete sensory sentence>.
- Type pool — display: <2-4 Google Fonts + weights>. Body: <2-3>.
- Palette derivation: <how to derive from brand/world, temperature, where the accent goes>
- Topology: <hero approach, section patterns, the list/grid treatment>
- Motion (<n-n>): <the motion personality in one line>
- Texture: <optional, one or two moves>
- Avoid: <the direction's own internal clichés>
```
